Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our courses and programs

Is this course suitable for complete beginners?

Our courses are designed for complete beginners. We start with the basics and gradually progress to advanced topics. No prior experience in digital marketing is required. Our expert instructors will guide you through every step of the learning process.

What is the duration of the complete program?

The complete digital marketing program is 6 months long with flexible timing options. Individual courses range from 1.5 to 3 months. We offer both weekday and weekend batches to accommodate working professionals and students.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes! We provide 100% job placement assistance with our guarantee program. Our dedicated placement team works with 800+ partner companies. We also provide interview preparation, resume building, and salary negotiation support. Our placement rate is 98%.

Are the classes live or recorded?

All our classes are live and interactive. We believe in real-time learning with instant doubt resolution. However, all sessions are recorded and available for review. You can access the recordings anytime through our student portal.

What certifications will I receive?

You'll receive industry-recognized certifications from Google, Facebook, HubSpot, and other leading platforms. Additionally, you'll get a SkillKool completion certificate. These certifications are highly valued by employers and significantly boost your resume.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our training quality within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund. No questions asked. We're confident in our training quality and student success rate.

Do you provide lifetime support?

We provide lifetime support to all our students. This includes career guidance, industry updates, access to our alumni network, and continued mentorship. Our relationship with students doesn't end after course completion.

What tools and software access do I get?

You get free access to premium marketing tools worth ₹50,000+ including SEMrush Pro, Ahrefs Standard, Canva Pro, Mailchimp, Hootsuite, and many more. These tools are essential for practical learning and real-world application.
